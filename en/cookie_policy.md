## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.

## 3. Types of cookies we use

### 3.1. Technical or essential cookies

These are cookies that allow the user to navigate through the platform and use the different options or services that exist on it. They are essential for the operation of the platform and do not require your consent.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| session_id | Keep the user session active | Session |
| auth_token | Authentication and access security | Session |
| csrf_token | Protection against CSRF attacks | Session |
| preferences | Store user preferences (language, theme) | 1 year |

### 3.2. Analytics cookies

These allow tracking and analysis of user behavior on the platform. The information collected is used to improve the service. These cookies require your prior consent.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics | Distinguish unique users | 2 years |
| _gid | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 24 hours |
| _gat | Google Analytics | Limit request rate | 1 minute |

**Google Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to obtain aggregate statistics about platform usage. Google may transfer this information to third parties when required by law, or when such third parties process the information on behalf of Google. For more information, see the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 4. Legal basis for using cookies

- **Technical/essential cookies:** Do not require consent as they are necessary for the provision of the requested service (Art. 22.2 LSSI).
- **Analytics cookies:** Are installed with prior user consent (Art. 22.2 LSSI and GDPR Art. 6.1.a).

## 5. How can you manage cookies?

### 5.1. Platform settings

When you first access the platform, you will be shown a notice about the use of cookies where you can:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Configure your preferences

You can modify your preferences at any time from your account settings.

### 5.2. Browser settings

You can also configure your browser to accept or reject all cookies, or to alert you when a cookie is being installed. Links to manage cookies in the most common browsers are:

- [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

**Important:** Disabling technical cookies may prevent the platform from functioning properly.
